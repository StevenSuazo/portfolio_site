About Me

Educator turned Software Engineer. I've been passionate about Software Development ever since I changed the background color on my MySpace page. I knew I'd found a new hobby. I got a degree in childhood education, but also got a part-time job at Apple Retail. Becoming a "Genius" and immersing myself in the environments of technology sparked my passion for Programming again.

After 4 short weeks at an App Academy prep course, I was absolutely hooked. I took a risk and left my job to enroll in a full-time 16 week immersive bootcamp program at App Academy. This was one of the scariest and best decisions I've made in my life, but now I have learned more than I ever imagined. I have graduated from an intensive bootcamp with < 3% acceptance rate, created web apps from scratch, worked on a team and collaborated on projects using a Github workflow, and many more things I never though I'd be doing. This once hobby of mine has truly become a lifelong passion.
